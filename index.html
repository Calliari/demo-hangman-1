Hangman game instructions

A secret word will be chosen automatically. The word will be converted into dashes, so the player can see how many letters at least, 'Hint'! The player tries to guess the word pressing one of the letters using the virtual keypad, once a time. If the player gets the wright letter it will revel from the dashes. If the player gets it wrong perts of the "hanman" will be drawing from every wrong guess. When the player guess it 6 times wrong the whole "hangman" body will be complete, so GAME OVER! And if the player complete the word with less than 6 wrong guesses the complete word will show and the player wins!
